## 🚨 Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability within any utility in this DevKit, we appreciate your **Ethical Intent** in reporting it responsibly.

### 📬 How to Report
Please **do not** open a public issue. Instead, follow the sovereign path of private disclosure:
1.  **Email:** Send a detailed report to [Your Email/Contact] with the subject: `SECURITY VULNERABILITY: [Tool Name]`.
2.  **Details:** Include a description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, and its potential impact on digital sovereignty.

### ⏳ Our Commitment
*   **Acknowledgment:** We will acknowledge your report within **48 hours**.
*   **Resolution:** We aim to provide a fix or mitigation strategy within **7 business days**.
*   **Recognition:** With your permission, we will honor your contribution in our release notes as a **Guardian of the Sovereign Infrastructure**.
